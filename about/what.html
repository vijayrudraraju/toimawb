what/

this is a space for me to play with some ideas. seeing as a greater and greater percentage of computers are interacted with through touchscreens about 100mm x 50mm in size, im interested in finding out what the maximum amount of creative functionality that can be accessed through such a device is. constraints include being able to fit the whole system on a mobile device and every interface being clearly visible and usable purely through human fingers on a small touchscreen without using hacks like scrolling, tabs, modes, etc.
