what/

this is a space for me to play with the idea of what i call "toi machines". toi machines are virtual machines. not virtual machines in the sense of the java runtime or some other similarly obtuse machine, but "virtual machines" in the sense that they are controllable dynamic systems made up of multiple parts, that generate some dynamical behavior. the perceived dynamic behavior is visual and the visuals function simultaneously as the controls to the system. the word "toi" conveys the whimsical nature of the machines and the hope that they are fun to play with and engender interest.
